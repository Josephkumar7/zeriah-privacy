Privacy Policy for Zeriah AI

Last updated: December 7, 2025

Zeriah AI ("the App") is developed by Joseph Kumar. This Privacy Policy explains how the App collects, uses, stores, and protects user data.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

We collect and store the following information when you create an account:

  • Email address
  • Password (securely hashed — we cannot view your actual password)
  • Google account basic profile information (if you choose “Login with Google”), including:
    • Google-provided name
    • Google-provided email

We do not access any other Google account data.

1.2 Profile and Preference Information

To personalize your experience in Zeriah AI, we may collect information such as:

  • The platforms you are interested in creating social media content for (for example, Instagram, YouTube, etc.).
  • The content types you are interested in (for example, short-form videos, long-form videos, posts, carousels, etc.).
  • Your current or target audience size.
  • Your goals for the next 6 months while using the app (for example, growth, monetization, consistency, engagement).

We use this information only to tailor recommendations, suggestions and features inside the app to better fit your needs and goals.

1.3 User-Generated Content

We store the content you generate using the App, including:

  • Captions
  • Keywords
  • Descriptions
  • Scripts
  • Any AI-generated output you save or use inside your account

This is required so your history and generated content remain available in your account.

1.4 Automatically Collected Data

Through advertising SDKs, third-party networks may collect information such as:

  • Device identifiers
  • IP address
  • App usage statistics
  • Approximate location (for ad relevance)

This data collection is performed by Google AdMob, Unity Ads, and mediation partners under their own privacy policies.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Create and maintain user accounts.
  • Authenticate logins (email/password or Google login).
  • Generate and display AI-based content and save your generated results.
  • Personalize your in-app experience based on your selected platforms, content types, audience size and goals.
  • Improve app performance, features and user experience.
  • Serve ads (personalized or non-personalized depending on user settings and applicable law).

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Advertisements

Zeriah AI uses the following advertising services:

  • Google AdMob
  • Unity Ads
  • Mediation networks integrated through AdMob

These services may collect device-level information needed to deliver and optimize ads. Their privacy policies can be found at:

  • Google AdMob:  https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Unity Ads:  https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy

4. Data Storage & Security

4.1 Storage

  • Account data is stored securely on our servers.
  • Passwords are hashed and cannot be read by us.
  • User-generated content and profile/preference information are stored so you can access them through your account and receive personalized recommendations.

4.2 Security Measures

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures such as:

  • HTTPS encryption
  • Secured authentication
  • Restricted server access

While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Children’s Privacy

Zeriah AI is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has created an account or provided personal information, we will take steps to delete that account and associated data.
